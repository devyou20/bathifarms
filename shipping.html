Shipping Policy

Last updated: August 27, 2025

1. Delivery Areas

Bathi Farms currently delivers to the following areas in Karnataka, India:

  • Davanagere City and nearby areas - Same day delivery available
  • Harihar, Honnali, Channagiri - Next day delivery
  • Shivamogga, Hubballi-Dharwad, Bengaluru - Delivery within 2 business days
  • Other regions across Karnataka - Delivery within 2-4 business days

To check if we deliver to your specific location, please enter your postcode on our checkout page or contact our customer service team.

2. Delivery Methods

2.1 Farm-Direct Delivery

Our own delivery service operating within Davanagere and surrounding areas. This premium service includes:

  • Temperature-controlled vehicles to maintain product freshness
  • Delivery by our trained staff who can answer questions about products
  • Specific time slots that you can select during checkout
  • Real-time tracking through SMS updates

2.2 Partner Courier Service

For deliveries outside our direct service area, we partner with trusted courier services:

  • Special packaging designed for perishable items
  • Priority handling and expedited shipping
  • Tracking information provided via email

2.3 Farm Pickup

You may also choose to pick up your order directly from our farm in Davanagere:

  • No delivery fees
  • Flexible pickup times between 9 AM and 5 PM, Monday through Saturday
  • Tour our facilities while you're here (by appointment)

3. Delivery Times

3.1 Delivery Days

We deliver Monday through Saturday. Orders placed:

  • Before 10 AM: May qualify for same-day delivery (Kandy area only)
  • After 10 AM: Will be processed for the next available delivery day
  • Weekend orders: Will be processed on the following Monday

3.2 Delivery Windows

During checkout, you can select from available delivery windows:

  • Morning (9 AM - 12 PM)
  • Afternoon (12 PM - 3 PM)
  • Evening (3 PM - 6 PM)

Our driver will call you 30 minutes before arrival within your selected window.

5. Perishable Products Policy

Due to the nature of our farm-fresh products:

  • We use special insulated packaging to maintain freshness
  • Products requiring refrigeration are packed with eco-friendly ice packs
  • Someone must be present to receive perishable deliveries
  • If no one is available to receive the delivery, we may leave it in a safe place if you've provided authorization

6. Delivery Delays

While we strive to meet all delivery timeframes, delays may occur due to:

  • Adverse weather conditions
  • Unexpected road closures or traffic
  • Natural disasters
  • Other circumstances beyond our control

In such cases, we will:

  • Contact you as soon as possible
  • Provide a revised delivery estimate
  • Offer options such as rescheduling or cancellation if needed

7. Missing or Damaged Items

If your order arrives with missing or damaged items:

  • Inspect all products upon delivery
  • Note any issues on the delivery receipt if possible
  • Contact us within 24 hours at support@bathifarms.com
  • Include photos of damaged items or packaging

Please refer to our Returns & Refunds Policy for more information on how we handle these situations.

8. International Shipping

At this time, we do not ship products internationally due to the perishable nature of our products and import restrictions on agricultural goods.
